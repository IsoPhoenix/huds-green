About HUDS Green

Why Use HUDS Green?

Beef and dairy account for 14.5% of the world’s greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions. In fact, if you usually eat beef once or twice a week, your consumption of beef is contributing to around 604kg of GHG emissions per year — that’s enough to drive a petrol car 1,542 miles or power the average UK home for 95 days!

So why don’t more people make the choice not to eat meat?

According to a 2019 study conducted by UCLA psychologists, the 5 key barriers to eating vegetarian are “perceptions that following a vegetarian diet would be (1) inadequately nutritious, (2) too financially expensive, (3) unfamiliar, (4) inconvenient, and (5) inadequately tasty.

Well, what if it was easy to know that you’re meeting your daily recommended protein intake — and conveniently available with your already-paid-for HUDS meal plan?

Actually, it is easy — just use HUDS Green!

How do we calculate how much protein you need?
We follow the Recommended Dietary Allowance (RDA) for protein which suggests we eat a modest 0.36 grams of protein per pound of body weight. Of course, if you are highly active or would just like to eat more protein than we suggest, that’s also fine!
